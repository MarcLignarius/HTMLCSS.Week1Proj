Why Programming?

Two experiences have led me to begin my programming journey.

  • Working at Squarespace, a popular website builder.

Squarespace provides software as a service for website building and hosting. Its customers use pre-built website templates and drag and drop elements to create webpages. While working there I always wondered how Squarespace's platform was coded to be able to handle so many complex changes (sometimes a complete site remodel) while providing this simple interface to its customers.

  • Working at Riot Games as the Product Owner of an application.

Riot Games is the producer of League of Legends, arguably one of the most popular video games of this decade. Working as a Product Owner for an application, I remember thinking in every sprint meeting "man, I really want to learn how to build something like this".

Learning how to program is also an excellent way of developing and cultivating a growth mindset, which is something I've struggled with at times.
